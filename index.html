about us

we make your fitness goals a reality

In 2020, the first Sigma Nation opened in Bengaluru in the South Of India. Over the next two years, Sigma Nation catered to over 420 members, making it a leader in the national fitness industry. We are now among the largest chain of fitness centres that offers energetic group workouts and multiple workout formats to choose from.

We are always looking for great people to join our team. We're committed to training, so you'll be encouraged to improve your existing skills while you develop new ones.
